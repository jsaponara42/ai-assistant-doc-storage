## Content

### Sense-Simulate-Act Framework

| Layer | Core question | AI modality | Typical outcome |
|---|---|---|---|
| Sense | What is changing right now? | Streaming ML on a unified data fabric | Network and operations anomalies detected up to 40% faster |
| Simulate | What could happen next? | Generative models that assemble rich scenarios | Planning cycles compressed from months to days |
| Act | What should we do automatically? | Agentic AI that orchestrates multistep workflows | Service restoration and cycle times reduced by double digits |

### Resilience Capacity x AI Amplifier

| Resilience capacity | Traditional enabler | AI amplifier |
|---|---|---|
| Absorptive | Slack resources and buffers | Predictive reallocators shift staffing and stock before shortages bite |
| Adaptive | Flexible structures and processes | Generative AI-authored playbooks reroute work in days rather than quarters |
| Regenerative | Learning culture after setbacks | Reinforcement learning agents automate postmortems and feed insights into code and process bases |

### Business Metric Uplift with an Integrated AI Stack

| Business metric | Typical uplift with an integrated AI stack |
|---|---|
| Forecast accuracy | Increase of ~12-15% through ensemble ML blended with generative AI narrative analysis |
| Working capital | Reduction of ~8-12% as AI tunes buffers and safety stock |
| Service-level adherence | Increase of ~15% as AI agents clear backlogs continuously |
| Time to market | Reduction of ~25-30% due to generative code assistance and autonomous testing |
| Regulatory and brand risk | Reduction of ~30% because large language models prescreen content before release |

### Adoption Roadmap (0-12 Months)

| Horizon | Milestone | Illustrative actions |
|---|---|---|
| Months 0-3 | Strategic clarity and data readiness | Value case workshop, latency and lineage audit, select two high-value generative AI pilots with clear baselines |
| Months 3-6 | Operational muscle | Stand up LLMOps, launch control tower dashboards, begin agentic proof of concept in a process with real orchestration needs |
| Months 6-12 | Institutional scale | Tie agent outcomes to KPIs and compensation, open an internal AI academy, publish a quarterly transparency report with usage and risk telemetry |
