## Summary

Apply Cialdini's principle of reciprocation to generate consulting leads by giving away free, high-value insight upfront — creating a sense of obligation that converts to paid engagements. Expand beyond PI law firms to a more receptive industry vertical.

## Context

PI law firms have proven difficult to market to. This idea emerged from frustration with that vertical and a desire to apply foundational principles (Cialdini's reciprocation, Alex Hormozi's lead-gen model, Steve Blank's customer discovery) to build pipeline more effectively.

## Content

**Core mechanic — reciprocation trigger:**
- Give something genuinely valuable for free (audit, insight, mini-consultation)
- The act of giving creates psychological obligation in the recipient
- Follow up with an offer; people who received value are far more likely to engage

**Tactical options:**
1. **Free audit / lead magnet** — deliver a workflow waste snapshot or industry-specific insight piece; use it as the cold outreach hook
2. **Raffle / prize consultation** — offer a free consultation (valued at $XYZ) as a prize draw; generates leads and creates reciprocation with all entrants
   - Non-winners still receive a discount offer → captures the reciprocation effect for the broader list (Hormozi-style recontact)

**Go-to-market pivot:**
- Move away from PI law as the primary target; explore other SMB verticals that are more receptive to outbound marketing
- Use Steve Blank customer discovery methodology: conduct interviews across multiple industries, deliver free insight during the interview, identify who responds best
- Goal: find a beachhead vertical, get initial consulting projects, build cashflow, then productize

**Influences:**
- Robert Cialdini — *Influence* (reciprocation principle)
- Alex Hormozi — lead-gen and recontact sequences
- Steve Blank — customer discovery / get out of the building
