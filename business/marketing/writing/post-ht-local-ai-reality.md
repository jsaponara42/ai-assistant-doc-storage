## Summary
Running AI locally is getting better, but the math still favors cloud for most people. A capable local setup costs significant money upfront vs. per-token or subscription costs. For the vast majority, cloud services are better, more capable, and more user friendly. Local AI is a hobby for those with patience and a knack for tinkering.
