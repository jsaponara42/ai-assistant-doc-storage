# Statement of Work

**Blue Tusk LLC & Maycomb Capital**

Date: June 16, 2026
Client Contact: Ariella Rotenberg
Blue Tusk Contact: John-Carlos Saponara (jcsaponara@bluetuskllc.com)

## Project Overview

Blue Tusk will deliver a complimentary AI Roadmap Consultation for Maycomb Capital, identifying where AI and automation can reduce manual work across the firm's operations and providing a prioritized, ROI-oriented plan for what to act on first.

## Scope of Work

**Engagement:** AI Roadmap Consultation (Free)

Blue Tusk will conduct discovery with Maycomb Capital and deliver a written AI Roadmap covering:

- A discovery conversation mapping Maycomb's operational pain points, both internal and external
- A working definition of Maycomb's longer-term vision for AI and automation across the firm
- Identification and prioritization of automation opportunities, ranked by ease of implementation and impact
- An AI Roadmap document with written recommendations and expected ROI for each opportunity

**Format:**  2 to 4 working sessions — an intro call, 1-2 discovery sessions, and a session to walk through the completed Roadmap.

**Deliverable:** Written AI Roadmap document, delivered digitally.

**Optional add-on:** If a sufficiently small, well-defined quick win is identified during discovery, Blue Tusk may offer to build it at no charge, at Blue Tusk's sole discretion.

## What's Not Included

This engagement is strategic and advisory only. It does not include:

- Implementation, build, or deployment of any automation (outside of an optional quick win offered at Blue Tusk's discretion)
- Ongoing support, monitoring, or maintenance
- Any third-party tool setup, licensing, or configuration

Implementation of roadmap recommendations, and ongoing AI/automation advisory support, are available as separate, paid engagements under their own Statements of Work. This can include an ongoing advisory retainer — a standing resource for AI and automation questions, oversight of automations already in place, and continued direction toward Maycomb's broader vision.

## Timeline

Roadmap delivery is targeted within 2–3 weeks of the discovery session, contingent on timely scheduling and stakeholder availability.

## Governing Terms

This Statement of Work operates under the Master Services Agreement (MSA) between Blue Tusk LLC and Maycomb Capital. The MSA will be sent separately for signature and governs all terms and conditions applicable to this engagement, including confidentiality, liability, and ownership.
