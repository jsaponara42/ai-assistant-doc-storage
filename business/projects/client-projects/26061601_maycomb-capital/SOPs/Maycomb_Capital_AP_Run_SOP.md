## 1. Purpose

This SOP documents the end-to-end process for Maycomb Capital's biweekly Accounts Payable (AP) run. It covers how vendor invoices are collected, verified, allocated, approved, and ultimately paid. The goal is to ensure that all business expenses are paid accurately and on time, with appropriate financial controls in place.

This document is written for someone stepping into the Finance & Operations Associate role with a general understanding of business finance, but no prior knowledge of Maycomb Capital's specific systems or workflows.

## 2. Background & Key Concepts

**What is an AP Run?**
An AP run (Accounts Payable run) is the process of collecting all outstanding bills the company owes to outside vendors, verifying them, getting them approved, and releasing payment. Maycomb Capital runs this process every two weeks.

**What is Ultimus Leverpoint?**
Ultimus Leverpoint is Maycomb Capital's fund administrator — an external firm that handles back-office financial operations. In the AP process, they collect all invoices forwarded to them, prepare the AP list and allocations spreadsheet, set up payments in the banking portal, and release payments once approved.

**What are Allocations?**
Because Maycomb Capital operates a management company and multiple fund vehicles, each invoice must be assigned (allocated) to the correct fund or entity and the correct expense category (e.g., Legal, Rent, Conference). This is tracked in the Allocations Spreadsheet. Amounts owed by the funds to the management company appear in a "Due From" category.

**What is Sharefile?**
Sharefile is a secure file-sharing platform used to exchange documents with Ultimus Leverpoint. Ultimus uploads the AP list, PDF invoices, and allocations spreadsheet to a shared Sharefile folder for review.

**What is ACH / Wire?**
These are the two methods used to send electronic payments. ACH (Automated Clearing House) is typically used for domestic payments and takes 1–2 business days. Wire transfers are faster and used for larger or time-sensitive payments. Both are set up by Ultimus Leverpoint and released from the Wells Fargo portal.

## 5. Schedule

The AP run occurs biweekly, typically on the 1st and 3rd Fridays of each month. The cycle spans approximately two weeks:
- Weeks 1–2: Invoices are received and forwarded to Ultimus Leverpoint on a rolling basis.
- End of Week 2 (Friday): Ultimus sends the AP list via Sharefile for review.
- Following business days: Review, back-and-forth corrections, MD approval, and payment release.

If a scheduled Friday falls on a holiday or there are unusual circumstances, coordinate with Ultimus Leverpoint to adjust the timeline.

## 6. Step-by-Step Process

The AP run is broken into 8 steps below. Each step includes what you need, where to find it, what systems you'll use, who is responsible, and what you produce.

### Step 1: Receive & Forward Invoices to Ultimus Leverpoint
| Field | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| **Inputs** | Vendor invoices (in PDF or other formats) received via email. |
| **Where inputs come from** | Invoices arrive by email to Danielle and/or Varenka directly from vendors, conference organizers, or other external parties Maycomb Capital does business with. |
| **Software & tools** | Email (Outlook) |
| **Who is responsible** | Finance & Operations Associate (Danielle or Varenka) |
| **Outputs** | Each invoice forwarded to Ultimus Leverpoint via email. Internal tracker updated with invoice details. |
| **Where outputs go** | Ultimus Leverpoint (by email); internal Excel tracker on SharePoint. |
| **Notes** | Forward invoices to Ultimus as soon as they are received — do not wait until the end of the cycle. This ensures nothing is missed. Also log the invoice in the internal Excel tracker at the same time (see Step 2). |

### Step 2: Log Invoices in the Internal Tracker
| Field | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| **Inputs** | Invoice details: vendor name, invoice date, invoice amount, expense category. |
| **Where inputs come from** | From each invoice email received. Log the invoice at the same time as forwarding to Ultimus (Step 1). |
| **Software & tools** | Microsoft Excel (stored on Maycomb Capital SharePoint) |
| **Who is responsible** | Finance & Operations Associate (Danielle or Varenka) |
| **Outputs** | Updated internal Excel invoice tracker with all invoices received during the two-week cycle. |
| **Where outputs go** | Saved to Maycomb Capital SharePoint. Used in Step 4 to cross-check Ultimus's AP list. |
| **Notes** | The tracker serves as an independent second record. It is critical for catching any invoices that Ultimus may have missed or recorded incorrectly. |

### Step 3: Receive AP List & Supporting Documents from Ultimus Leverpoint
| Field | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| **Inputs** | Notification from Ultimus Leverpoint that the biweekly AP list is ready for review. |
| **Where inputs come from** | Ultimus sends a Sharefile link via email at the end of each two-week cycle (typically the 1st or 3rd Friday of the month). The Sharefile folder contains three items: (1) AP List in Excel format, (2) a folder of all individual invoices in PDF format, and (3) the Allocations Spreadsheet in Excel format. |
| **Software & tools** | Email (Outlook); Sharefile |
| **Who is responsible** | Finance & Operations Associate (Danielle or Varenka) — receives and opens documents. |
| **Outputs** | Three documents downloaded/accessed from Sharefile: AP List (Excel), PDF invoice folder, Allocations Spreadsheet (Excel). |
| **Where outputs go** | Reviewed locally. Used in Step 4 for verification. |

### Step 4: Review & Verify the AP List, PDFs, and Allocations
| Field | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| **Inputs** | (1) Ultimus AP List (Excel), (2) PDF invoice folder, (3) Allocations Spreadsheet (Excel) — all from Sharefile. (4) Internal invoice tracker from SharePoint. |
| **Where inputs come from** | Sharefile (documents from Ultimus); Maycomb Capital SharePoint (internal tracker). |
| **Software & tools** | Microsoft Excel (for AP List and Allocations); PDF viewer (for invoice PDFs); Sharefile |
| **Who is responsible** | Finance & Operations Associate (Danielle or Varenka) |
| **Outputs** | Verified AP List and Allocations Spreadsheet. A list of any discrepancies or correction requests sent to Ultimus via email. |
| **Where outputs go** | Corrections communicated to Ultimus by email. Ultimus updates documents in Sharefile. Review this updated version before proceeding to Step 5. |
| **Notes** | Three checks to complete: (1) Cross-check Ultimus AP List against your internal tracker — every invoice should appear on both. (2) Open each PDF invoice and confirm the amount matches what is listed in the AP List. (3) Review the Allocations Spreadsheet — confirm each invoice is assigned to the correct expense category (e.g., Legal, Rent) and the correct fund/entity. Invoices shared across funds will appear in a 'Due From' allocation. If anything is incorrect, email Ultimus with the specific corrections. They will update the files in Sharefile. Repeat this check until everything is accurate. If a discrepancy cannot be resolved, escalate to the Managing Director. |

### Step 5: Send AP List & Allocations to Managing Director for Final Approval
| Field | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| **Inputs** | Finalized AP List (Excel) and Allocations Spreadsheet (Excel) from Sharefile, confirmed accurate after Step 4. |
| **Where inputs come from** | Sharefile (Ultimus's updated documents). |

| **Outputs** | Slack to Miljana with the AP List and Allocations Spreadsheet attached, requesting final sign-off. |

| **Notes** | The Managing Director performs a final check that all invoices are present, amounts are correct, and allocations make sense. Once they confirm approval (by slack), you can proceed. |

### Step 6: Confirm Approval to Ultimus Leverpoint & Await Payment Setup
| Field | Detail |
| --- | --- |

| **Where inputs come from** | Managing Director's slack reply. |

| **Who is responsible** | Finance & Operations Associate (Danielle or Varenka) |

### Step 7: Verify Payments in Wells Fargo & Notify Management Team
| Field | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| **Inputs** | (1) Confirmation email from Ultimus that payments are set up in Wells Fargo. (2) Approved AP List (Excel) from Step 4/5. |
| **Where inputs come from** | Ultimus confirmation email; Sharefile (AP List). |
| **Software & tools** | Wells Fargo Online Portal (View Only — individual credentials); Slack |
| **Who is responsible** | Finance & Operations Associate (Danielle or Varenka) |
| **Outputs** | (1) Verified that all payments in Wells Fargo match the approved AP List. (2) Slack message sent to the full management team summarizing the AP run and attaching the AP List. |

| **Notes** | Log into Wells Fargo using your own individual credentials (View Only access). Compare each payment set up in the portal against the approved AP List — verify vendor name and dollar amount for every line item. The Slack message to management should include: total number of ACH payments and total dollar amount, total number of wire payments and total dollar amount, any unusual payments or items that warrant awareness, and the full AP List attached as a file. |

### Step 8: Management Approval in Wells Fargo & Payment Release Confirmation
| Field | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| **Inputs** | Slack message from Step 7 with AP List attached. |
| **Where inputs come from** | Slack (management team channel). |
| **Software & tools** | Wells Fargo Online Portal (management team members log in with their own credentials); Slack |
| **Who is responsible** | Two members of the management team — any two members with portal access. Finance & Operations Associate confirms release. |
| **Outputs** | (1) Each payment approved by two management team members in Wells Fargo. (2) Management team members slack confirmation. (3) Finance & Operations Associate confirms all payments released. |
| **Where outputs go** | Finance & Operations Associate confirms payment release with Ultimus Leverpoint by email. |
| **Notes** | Each individual payment requires approval from two separate management team members — this is a financial control requirement. Once both approvals are in place, the payments are released. Log into Wells Fargo (View Only) to confirm all payments show as released. Then email Ultimus to confirm. This completes the AP run cycle. The next cycle begins on the following 1st or 3rd Friday. |

| Document | Naming Convention |

| Internal Invoice Tracker | Stored on Maycomb Capital SharePoint. Follow existing folder structure. |

## 8. Exception Handling

**Discrepancy Between Internal Tracker and Ultimus AP List**
If an invoice appears on your internal tracker but not on the Ultimus AP List (or vice versa), flag this to Ultimus Leverpoint by email with the specific invoice details. Ultimus will investigate and update the AP list as needed. Do not proceed to Step 5 until all discrepancies are resolved.

**Incorrect Invoice Amount or Allocation**
Email Ultimus with the corrected information. Ultimus will update the documents in Sharefile. Re-review the updated documents before sending to the Managing Director.

**Managing Director Unavailable for Approval**
The AP List must be approved by a Managing Director or Partner before payments are set up. If Miljana is unavailable, escalate to another MD/Partner. Do not proceed without written approval.

**Unusual or Unexpected Invoice**
Flag this in the Slack message to management (Step 7) so they are aware before approving. If you are uncertain whether an invoice is legitimate, raise it with the Managing Director before including it in the AP run.
