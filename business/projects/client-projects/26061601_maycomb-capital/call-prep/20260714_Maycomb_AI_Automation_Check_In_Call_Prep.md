If Andi isn't in the room, or doesn't get a dedicated readout some other way, there's a real risk this stalls indefinitely at "let me check with Andi."

Some of what's in the roadmap touches budget decisions, so it's worth having whoever holds that authority see it directly rather than secondhand. That's a legitimate reason on its own, and it also happens to be exactly what the upsell needs.
