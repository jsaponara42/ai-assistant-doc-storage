The Playbook governance items are resolved, so this slot is free. Worth using it to float the AI-steward idea with Martina and Barry, as a reaction check, not a pitch, no numbers, no ask.

### 3. Setting up the final readout (15–20 min) — the actual point of this call

This is where the full written roadmap and the presentation get delivered, and functionally, it's also where any next-phase engagement gets decided. Getting the right people in that room matters more than the mechanics of scheduling it.

**The one thing worth pushing for: Andi Phillips.**

Per the Budgeting SOP, Andi approves all budgets. She hasn't been part of a single conversation in this engagement so far. Nothing in the roadmap, the steward retainer, the quick wins bundle, or anything past it, can move forward without her (or someone she explicitly delegates to) actually seeing the work. Martina can champion this internally, but her own spending authority has never been confirmed. If Andi isn't in the room, or doesn't get a dedicated readout some other way, there's a real risk this stalls indefinitely at "let me check with Andi."

**How to raise it without it sounding like a sales tactic:** it's true, so say the true thing. Some of what's in the roadmap touches budget decisions, so it's worth having whoever holds that authority see it directly rather than secondhand. That's a legitimate reason on its own, and it also happens to be exactly what the upsell needs.

**Other attendees:**
- **Barry** — yes. His role touches admin/governance and several roadmap items need LeverPoint coordination he's closest to.
- **Martina** — yes. Primary contact, likely champion for at least two of the four upsell tracks.
- **Danielle** — probably not necessary. She's exiting operational relevance by end of August and won't be implementing anything from the roadmap. Her value is fully captured in this call and the SOPs already delivered. Don't include her out of politeness if it's not adding anything for her.
- **The incoming Investor Relations hire** — starts August 3. Too early to invite to a call before he's started, but P-007 (outcome data / impact reporting) is his eventual problem to inherit. Worth proposing a separate, later touchpoint with him specifically, rather than folding him into this one.

**Timing:** propose a specific window instead of leaving it open. Two to three weeks out gives enough runway to finish the Gamma deck and the branding pass on the written roadmap without losing momentum.
