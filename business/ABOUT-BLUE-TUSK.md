## Positioning

### Against DIY tools (Lawmatics, Clio, etc.)
Done-for-you vs. do-it-yourself. Clients don't have time to learn and implement.

### Against hiring staff
One-time setup cost vs. ongoing salaries, benefits, training. Scales better.

### Against doing nothing
Quantify the cost of lost leads and wasted time.

### Key messages
- "We're not just here to automate — we're here to modernize, innovate, and partner with your business for the long run."
- "Think of us as your modernization and innovation partner — bringing both technical expertise and business insight."
- "Measurable ROI, not hype."
