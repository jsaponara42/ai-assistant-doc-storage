## Summary
Finalized entertainment plan for a Tuscan hillside outdoor wedding at sunset. Three areas: after-midnight party accessories, bilingual table card with games and scavenger hunt, and post-wedding photo sharing.

## Context
- **Venue:** Outdoor, Tuscan hills, sunset setting
- **Format:** Long Italian dinner (multi-hour), guests walk around and mingle
- **Constraint:** No reliable internet connection in the hills
- **Languages:** English + Italian (bilingual required for table card)

---

### 1. 🕛 After-Midnight Party Accessories

| Item | Notes |
|---|---|

| LED foam glow sticks | More durable through the night |
| Feather boas | Assorted colors |
| Socks | Handed out right before going down to the party room |

---

### 2. 🎲 Table Card (Double-Sided, One Per Table, EN + IT)

#### Two Truths & a Lie / *Due Verità, Una Bugia*
Each person says 3 things about themselves — two true, one false. The table guesses which is the lie.

*Ognuno al tavolo dice tre cose su di sé — due vere, una falsa. Il tavolo indovina qual è la bugia.*

---

### 3. 📷 Photo Sharing
Post-wedding Google Drive link sent to all guests once back in range.

### BACK SIDE

---

**John-Carlos & Viviana Trivia / *Trivia su John-Carlos e Viviana***

*How well do you know the couple? Discuss your answers — the MC will reveal them later.*
*Quanto conosci gli sposi? Discutete le risposte — il MC le svelerà più tardi.*

1. Where did John-Carlos and Viviana first meet? / *Dove si sono incontrati per la prima volta?*
2. What was their first trip together? / *Qual è stato il loro primo viaggio insieme?*
3. What city did they have their first date in? / *In quale città hanno avuto il loro primo appuntamento?*
4. What is Viviana's go-to comfort food? / *Qual è il cibo preferito di Viviana nei momenti di comfort?*

---

**Conversation Starters / *Spunti di Conversazione***

*Share your answers with the table.*
*Condividi le tue risposte con il tavolo.*
